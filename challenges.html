Drug Development Financing

Too high Costs of Drug Development

  • The average drug development cost in prevalent diseases is around USD 1.5 billion and rising. One way to reduce costs in the short to mid term is to bring some parts of the clinical development into Asia or Eastern Europe and use more artificial intelligence aspects.
